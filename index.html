In this project, I utilized SQL to explore a comprehensive COVID-19 dataset, focusing on uncovering insights and analyzing correlations. I wrote queries to extract valuable insights on infection rate by date, regions, and population.

A significant part of the project involved examining the correlation between deaths and vaccination counts.
